FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do I get paid per sign-up?

No — only when your referral makes a real payment. Then it's €30 (Starter) or €100 (Growth), or 20% of the annual contract for an Enterprise deal.

When and how do I get paid?

The first week of each month, for everything that converted the month before.

Is there any cost?

None. You even get a free Starter account — yours as long as you bring at least one paying client a month.

What's an Enterprise deal?

A larger custom contract. You bring the company in and stay involved through the close, with Nova's team backing you on demos and expertise, and you earn 20% of the annual contract when it's signed.

Do I have to use Nova Recruiter myself?

You get it free — and you'll pitch it far better once you've used it on your own roles.

How is a referral tracked?

Your personal promo code. Anyone who signs up with it is credited to you.

Is it exclusive?

Not at all. Do it alongside your job or agency, and refer companies anywhere — it's global.
